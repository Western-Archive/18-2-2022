Welcome to the Western Weekly! We are currently the biggest server that surrounds providing news and information to the Wild West community! In this channel, you will find all of the necessary information that you will need, incase you find yourself lost and confused on how things work around here. Our history The Western Weekly was founded on November 29th, 2020 by Smusher, and Twisted, who are now both retired from the community. After Smusher and Twisted retired, Smusher appointed a new owner on May 3rd, 2021 known as Lewiss. Later on, Lewiss was terminated from his owner position as he was not doing a great job. So, later the staff decided that there should be no owner for Western Weekly, and came to the conclusion of appointing two server managers, known as Darth, and Just an Otter. Otter retired after a while, and Darth continued his duties. Later on, Smusher appointed another new Server Manager known as Doc, who then retired a month later. Darth retired a long time later, and gave the server back to Smusher.
⠀ Channels If you're a new user that does not know where to go, then this category will surely help you. #announcements - A channel where important information is distributed to the public. #rules - This channel provides all the rules and guidelines for the server. This can come in handy to prevent any future warns, kicks or maybe even bans. #roles - A channel in which you can gain roles yourself without asking for them. It is recommended to check out this channel. #polls - A channel which lets users vote for features which may be added to Western Weekly, or removed from Western Weekly. #faction-recruitment - A channel which allows users (specifically faction high ranks) to post recruitment messages for their factions. #business-partners - A channel which lists all of our current business partners and invites to their servers. #faction-partners - Where faction partners are listed along with their server invites. #qotd - A channel which users can give out fun, and wholesome questions for users to answer or provide their opinions on. #youtube-uploads - A channel where Wild West content creators can post their new content.
⠀ News News is one of the sole reasons on why Western Weekly exists, and is what made it popular until the day you are reading this. #articles - A channel in which our writer write and publish articles about the faction community. #starboard-announcements - A channel that shows all the important announcements, sneak peeks and updates and Starboard Studios release. #wlc-map - A channel that shows updates to Western Land Co's map. #faction-updates - A channel which shows updates that factions publish to the community. #other-updates - A channel where you can find updates from servers that aren't factions. #breaking-news - A channel where important information is published as soon as it happens. #coffee-news - A channel which publishes news on smaller factions every morning. #flash-news - A channel that provides all the important headlines and articles for users to read. ⠀ Public Category These are the channels that you can interact with other members in. #general - General chat. #discussion - A channel where you can go to discuss anything other than the Wild West (or argue about anything related to it). #deposit-info - Where you can send information to our writers and reporters who may decide to put it in #coffee-news or #flash-news. #feedback - Where you open a ticket to ask questions or just address public issues that revolve around the community.
⠀ ⠀ Server Roles These are all the current staff and member roles of the server. Starting from the very top, to the bottom. Management @Server Manager - The overseer of all operations which go on out throughout The Western Weekly. @Administrator - Second in command right under Editor in Chief. They also oversee all operations that run throughout the server, and make changes which benefit the community. @Head of Moderation - Third in command right under Administrators, they oversee all actions which moderators do throughout the server. @Head of the Faction Program - The person who occupies this role is in-charge of our faction partners. @Head of Reporters - The person who occupies this role is in-charge of giving tasks, or monitoring how reporters work. @Head of Writers - The person who occupies this role is in-charge of either reviewing the work which writers have done, or acting as a trainer towards the writers. Moderation @Senior Moderator - People who occupy this role are trusted by our admins, and has become a very good asset to our moderation team. @Server Moderator - These people help moderate our server everyday. To get this role, you can apply when our applications come out. @Trainee Moderator - These people are our newest addition to our moderation team, they have passed our applications and have been approved by our admins.
⠀ Staff @Reporter - These people report news to post in flash news and coffee news. @Editorial Writer - These people mainly write opinionated pieces based on current news in the Wild West community. @Writer - These people supply us with news articles for members to read, and enjoy throughout the server. @deleted-role - These people write articles for #flash-news. ⠀ ⠀ FAQ Q: How do I become a staff member? A: You may apply when applications come out in #announcements. Q: How do I get the "Honored Guest" role? A: You either have to be an important person to the community or be liked and well respected by others. Q: How do I report a member or a staff member? A: Message an admin or higher. Q: How can I access #faction-recruitment and #youtube-uploads? A: If you meet the requirements in #youtube-uploads, you can DM a head moderator or an admin to get your role. To get access to #faction-recruitment, you must open a ticket in #feedback. Q: How do I get my faction featured in #flash-news? A: You must report important information in #deposit-info, and our writers will decide if its good enough to go in the news.
