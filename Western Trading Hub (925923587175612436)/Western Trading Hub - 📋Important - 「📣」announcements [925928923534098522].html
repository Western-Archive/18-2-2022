Very important PSA A few people have gotten scammed/hacked over the past few days, and this is just a warning to be more careful. ALWAYS make sure of the following: - The links you click are legitimate, and are "roblox.com" instead of "so.roblox.com" - Never give anything called a ".Har file" to anyone, and do not accept any "Free GFX" for your roblox avatars - Links that look like a video SHOULD ALWAYS end in .mp4 or .mov And in the very rare occasion that you do accidentally click a scam link, reset your roblox cookies immediately by either logging out and logging back in, or refreshing your cookies with your browser. Have fun trading, and please stay safe! @everyone
